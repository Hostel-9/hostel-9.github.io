About The Event

About The Event

Hostel inmates, get ready! Nautanki (Read as 9tanki) is here. Nautanki is the Signature festival of Hostel 9, held annually. This time will be its 11th iteration, and will be held online for the first time given the lockdown restrictions due to the ongoing pandemic. Nautanki was a place where all inmates united, and celebrated the culture of our beloved hostel. Various Competitions, ranging from Call Of Duty, Poker, Meme God, Poetry and PaFA competiion to some fun loving StandUp Show, Improv Session and Movie Screening are going to be held with exciting prizes for the winners. Lockdown has significantly impacted the hostel culture, and Nautanki would be the perfect time everyone to reunite atleast virtually and enjoy as much as possible.
