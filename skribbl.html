Skribbl and Codenames

21 March. 8:00 PM onwards

Exciting Prizes worth 1500Rs

One game consists of a few rounds in which every round someone has to draw their chosen word and others have to guess it to gain points! The person with the most points at the end of the game will then be crowned as the winner!

Rules

  1. It’s an individual play.
  2. Registration and entry into the competition are FREE of cost
  3. Multiple Rounds will be played until a single winner is decided.
  4. All the players participating have to be on discord voice chat.
  5. Mentioning the word to be guessed or any suggestive letters is not allowed
  6. All players should join by their real name.(This should be strictly followed)
  7. Only hostel 9 residents are allowed to participate
  8. Link to the game will be shared on the Hostel 9 discord server(Link). All those interested to play should report by 7:50pm.

Two teams compete by each having a "spymaster" give one-word clues that can point to multiple words on the board. The other players on the team attempt to guess their team's words while avoiding the words of the other team. In a variant with 2–3 players, one spymaster gives clues to the other player or players.

Rules

  1. Teams will be made randomly on the spot or on mutual agreement.
  2. Spymaster can use the exact word as the clue.
  3. When the spymaster gives a clue, his or her field operatives try to figure out what it means. They can debate it amongst themselves, but the spymaster must keep a straight face.
  4. Clue must only be one word.
  5. Under any dispute or discrepancy organizers will have final say in the matter.
  6. All players should join by their real name.(This should be strictly followed)
  7. Only hostel 9 residents are allowed to participate
  8. Link to the game will be shared on Gmeet where both the teams have to be present. All those interested to play should report by 7:50pm.
  9. Spymasters have to keep their video and audio on at all times
  10. Number of games will be decided on the number of entries.
